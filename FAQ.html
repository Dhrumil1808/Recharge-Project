Queries regarding Recharge

Q. What to do if recharge is not done?


A.Wait for 5 minutes after the payment is successfully done. If your mobile number is still not recharged then please email us at support@OnlineRecharge.com and register a complaint. We will respond to it as soon as possible.



Q. Does the recharge happen immediately?


A. Yes. You get your recharge immediately. As soon as payment is made, you will get a confirmation mail from OnlineRecharge.com.After that, you can check balance in your phone. Also, you will get a message "Recharge has been done successfully" in your login page.



Q. Can I Recharge for my friends and relatives also?


A. Absolutely. You can recharge for your loved ones using the same account. You just need to know the mobile number to make a successful recharge.



Q. Is there any extra charge for recharging?


A. No, there is absolutely no extra charge for recharging. You will get same talk time what your mobile service provider offers you. We do not charge you anything extra for the recharge in the name of service charges, convenience charges or any other term.
